I Love Sphynx Cats

The Best Cats Ever

Canadian Sphynx is one of several hairless cat breeds. When creating the breed, a natural recessive mutation was fixed, leading to the absence of wool. At the moment, this is a fully formed and fairly stable breed with 50 years of experience, transmitting its traits in a recessive type.
